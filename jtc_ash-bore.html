Emerald Ash-Bore

An adult beetles nibble on the ash foliage and it causes little damage. The larvae feeds on the inner bark of the ash trees,disrupting the trees ability to transport water and nutrients. As of October 2018, the Emerald Ash-bore is found in 35 states of the U.S.A.
